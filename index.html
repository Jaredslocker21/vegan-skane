Two Monkeys review

Two Monkeys is a staple to Malmö where everyone wether you are Vegan or non Vegan can enjoy a handsome burger, wrap or Bowl. They are located at the Emporia Mall. Don't be fooled by being in a mall. Their food packs a lot of flavor.
